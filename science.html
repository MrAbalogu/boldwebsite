    What is Digital Therapy?

    Digital therapeutics (DTx), a subset of digital health, is a health discipline and treatment option that deliver evidence-based therapeutic interventions to patients that are driven by high-quality software programs to prevent, manage, or treat a medical disorder or disease. The treatment relies on behavioral and lifestyle changes usually spurred by a collection of digital impetuses. They are used independently or in concert with medications, devices, or other therapies to optimize patient care and health outcomes. Because of the digital nature of the methodology, data can be collected and analyzed as both a progress report and a preventative measure. DTx products incorporate advanced technology best practices relating to design, clinical validation, usability, and data security. They are reviewed and cleared or approved by regulatory bodies as required to support product claims regarding risk, efficacy, and intended use. Treatments are being developed for the prevention and management of a wide variety of diseases and conditions.

    Facts Based Answers

    Is digital therapeutics safe?

    Yes, they demonstrate safety and efficacy in randomized clinical trials. It also incorporate patient privacy and security protections

    Is digital therapeutics Evidence based?

    They deliver clinically-proven therapies that provide patients with effective self-management therapeutic options there expanding access to evidence-based medical therapies locally, nationally, and globally

    Do my healthcare provider cover its use?

    Because they can be prescribed to patients by qualified healthcare providers independently or in concert with medications, devices, or other therapies, they are reimbursed as products, via Pharmacy and Medical benefits

    Do regulatory bodies approve it?

    Can digital therapeutics be personalised?

    Yes they can, digital therapeutics provides the opportunity to produce personalize care based on individual patients’ needs and abilities

    Where do I administer digital therapeutics?

    They provide care independent of a patient’s schedule and in the privacy of their own environment (e.g., home, office, on-the-go, etc.)
